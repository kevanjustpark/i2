The AURA Framework

Aggregate. Understand. Recommend. Act.

JP Insights follows the AURA methodology - the industry's most comprehensive data aggregation layer, combined with JustPark AI, to close the loop from visibility to action.

A

Aggregate

Unify data from meters, PARCS, mobile payments, enforcement, LPR, and reservations into a single, real-time source of truth across every location.

U

Understand

JustPark AI - combining best-in-class LLMs with 12 years of Smarking expertise - identifies patterns, detects anomalies, and surfaces operational inefficiencies.

R

Recommend

Transforms fragmented data into clear, prioritized recommendations - from pricing adjustments and demand management to staffing optimization and policy changes.

A

Act

Execute recommendations directly with full transparency and control - or enable JustPark AI to automate approved decisions, adjusting pricing and operations in real time.

Recommend & Act

From insight to action, automatically

From dynamic pricing to anomaly detection, JP Insights automates the repetitive work - so your team can focus on strategy, compliance, and growth.

Automated

Dynamic Pricing Engine

Continuously optimizes rates based on demand, events, weather, and competitor pricing. Set your revenue goals and compliance guardrails - the system handles the rest.

Example: "When occupancy exceeds 85% and there's a concert within 2 miles, increase rate by 20% up to $25 max - with full audit trail."
Proactive

Smart Alerts & Anomaly Detection

Learns your normal patterns and alerts you only when something actually matters - not noise. Revenue drops, equipment failures, compliance breaches - caught instantly.

Example: "Revenue at Downtown Garage is 34% below expected for this time. This appears to be a gate malfunction - 3 lanes showing zero transactions."
Scheduled

Auto-Generated Reports

Automated weekly and monthly reports with narrative insights - not just numbers. Board-ready summaries, compliance reports, and public records documentation delivered to your inbox.

Example: "Your Q1 portfolio summary is ready. Key insight: Airport Garage outperformed by 18%, driven by increased airline capacity. Compliance report attached."
Predictive

Demand Forecasting

Predictive models forecast occupancy and revenue up to 30 days out - factoring in events, holidays, weather, and historical patterns. Plan staffing, pricing, and capacity with confidence.

Example: "Predicted 94% occupancy next Saturday due to Taylor Swift concert + home game. Recommend activating overflow lot and pre-selling 200 additional passes."
